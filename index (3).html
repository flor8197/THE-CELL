Quiz on "Cell Structure I – Nucleus"

1. What organelle is referred to as the "control center" of the cell?

✅ The nucleus stores the cell's DNA and regulates its activities.

2. Which molecule is stored inside the nucleus?

✅ The nucleus contains DNA, which holds genetic information.

3. What is the primary function of the nucleus?

✅ The nucleus regulates cell growth, metabolism, and reproduction.
